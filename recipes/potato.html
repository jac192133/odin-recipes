Potato

Description

This is a recipe for a tasty baked potato.

Ingredients

  • russet potato
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • pepper
  • butter
  • sour cream
  • chives

Steps

  1. preheat the oven to 400ËšF
  2. cover the potato in olive oil, salt, and pepper
  3. poke holes in the potato with a fork on both sides
  4. put potato on sheet pan and bake for 1 hour
  5. chop the chives
  6. remove, and make a deep slice along the top
  7. add butter, sour cream, chives, and enjoy
